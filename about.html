Who We Are
"MagicOfWords" is the world’s largest site for readers and book recommendations. Our mission is to help people find and share books they love. Our website was launched in April 2023.

A Few Things You Can Do On Our Site . . .
See what books your friends are reading. Track the books you're reading, have read, and want to read. Check out your personalized book recommendations. Find out if a book is a good fit for you from our community’s reviews.
